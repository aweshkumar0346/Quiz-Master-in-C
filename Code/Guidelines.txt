
                             GUIDELINES FOR GAME
                        ---------------------------


  -Choose Between FOUR Different Categories.
  -There Will Be THREE Rounds For Each Category.
  
  -In First Round:
  -You'll Receive 10 Points For Correct Answer.
  -You'll Lose 6 Points For Wrong Answer.
  
  -In Second Round:
  -You'll Receive 20 Points For Correct Answer.
  -You'll Lose 12 Points For Wrong Answer.

  -In Third Round:
  -You'll Receive 30 Points For Correct Answer.
  -You'll Lose 18 Points For Wrong Answer.

  -You'll Have One Lifeline For Each Rounds.
  -The Lifeline Will Allow You To Skip Question Without Losing Points.

  -Press Any Key For Main Menu.
